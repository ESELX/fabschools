Call for Educational Distributed Designs

Fablab Benfica at the Higher School of Education of the Polytechnic of Lisbon, the Distributed Design Market Platform - co-funded by the Creative Europe Program of the European Union and, the Fab Foundation would like to invite to submit your Open Educational Products and Resources

Challenge

The School is no longer "a building" that embeds and embodies knowledge, where it is transmitted and nurtured. That building is being "refurbished" every day to include a broader range of educational practices but also to open its gates wider and maybe to demolish its walls. The classroom is now everywhere (or has to be), and learning opportunities can be designed within that framework.

Prizes

We are expecting to select 10 projects which will be showcased in a final distributed exhibition that will tour the next editions of 4 featured European Maker Faires (Lisbon, Bilbao, Vienna and Barcelona) and be featured on both DistributedDesign.eu and Fabschools.pt catalog. Out of those 10 projects, we will select 2 to integrate our Distributed Design Summer School. The same 2 project teams will get a mentorship plan which will also cover further development and communication costs up to 500 € per project. Wikifactory prize: Free wikifactory subscription;
